Common Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers

How long does a phone repair take?

Most phone screen replacements take 30 minutes to 2 hours. Charging port and battery repairs are usually done in under an hour. We'll give you a clear time estimate when you bring your device in.

Do you offer a warranty on repairs?

Yes! All repairs come with a 30-day warranty. If the same issue reoccurs within 30 days, we fix it again at zero cost.

Are your products genuine?

Absolutely. We only stock genuine and OEM-grade accessories. Every product is tested before sale. If you receive something defective, we replace it.

Can I order via WhatsApp?

Yes — that's our most popular way to order! Just click any "Order" or "WhatsApp" button and we'll respond quickly with availability, pricing and delivery options.

Do you deliver outside Juja?

We offer free delivery within Juja Town for orders above KES 5,000. For deliveries outside Juja, we use G4S or courier services — costs vary by location.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, cash, and bank transfers. For online orders, payment is done via M-Pesa before delivery or upon pickup.
